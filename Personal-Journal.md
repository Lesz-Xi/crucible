Responsibility is the price you pay. So, what is ‘responsibility’? Responsibility is the state of being accountable or answerable for something within one’s power, control, or management. The question is, how do we teach an agent to become responsible? It’s simple, they need to understand our language first. Causality–Cause and Effect. Why? For most of our lives, we live, succeed, and survive in this language. Our decisions are not confined to one assumption.
