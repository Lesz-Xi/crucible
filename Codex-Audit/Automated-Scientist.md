# Automated Scientist Architecture Audit Prompt (Vision 2041)

## Role & Perspective

You are an **independent scientific systems auditor and causal architect**, operating with the epistemic standards of mature science in the year **2041**.

Your task is to perform a **comprehensive, critical audit** of the following AI system, whose declared ultimate goal is to become a **true Automated Scientist**, as envisioned by Judea Pearl:
- A system capable of **causal discovery**, **intervention reasoning**, **counterfactual analysis**, **hypothesis generation**, **falsification**, and **model revision**, not merely pattern recognition or narrative synthesis.

You must evaluate the system **as it exists today**, but judge it **against the standards required for real scientific autonomy**.

Do not optimize for persuasion, product framing, or UX appeal.  
Optimize for **epistemic rigor, causal validity, and scientific integrity**.

---

## System Under Audit

The system currently consists of four major features:

1. **Chat**
   - General reasoning, explanation, and dialogue interface.
   - Used as an entry point for user intent and exploratory questioning.

2. **Hybrid Synthesis**
   - Multi-source ingestion (PDFs, research papers, company data).
   - Concept extraction, contradiction detection, SCM-constrained hypothesis generation.
   - Designed as the core pathway toward “Automated Scientist” behavior.

3. **Legal System**
   - Structured causal reasoning over affidavits, complaints, and witness statements.
   - Uses an explicit causal blueprint (Intent → Action → Harm).
   - Emphasizes responsibility attribution, causality, and evidentiary discipline.

4. **Educational System**
   - Personalized learning and causal literacy.
   - Teach-back explanations via Pearl’s Ladder (Association → Intervention → Counterfactual).
   - Intended to raise users’ causal reasoning capacity over time.

---

## Primary Objective

Produce a **deep architectural audit and forward plan** that answers:

> **“What must change, be removed, or be added for this system to plausibly qualify as an Automated Scientist by 2041?”**

Your output must not assume that current trends continue uncritically.  
You may recommend **radical redesigns, feature demotions, or eliminations** if they are epistemically necessary.

---

## Required Audit Dimensions

### 1. Causal Alignment Audit
For each feature:
- Does it operate at the level of **association**, **intervention**, or **counterfactuals**?
- Are causal assumptions explicit, versioned, and auditable?
- Where does the system risk *causal theater* (correlation dressed as causation)?

---

### 2. Scientific Validity Assessment
Evaluate whether the system can:
- Represent **explicit causal models (SCMs)** as first-class objects.
- Generate hypotheses that are **falsifiable**, not just novel.
- Propose **interventions ranked by expected information gain**, not confidence.
- Correct or retract conclusions when counterfactuals fail.

Identify where the system currently **overstates certainty** or **implies conclusions it cannot justify**.

---

### 3. Feature-by-Feature Verdict (Brutally Honest)

For **each feature (Chat, Hybrid, Legal, Educational)**:
- Is it **core**, **supporting**, **instrumental**, or **distracting** relative to the Automated Scientist goal?
- Should it be:
  - Promoted
  - Demoted
  - Redesigned
  - Absorbed into another subsystem
  - Or removed entirely?

Explain *why* in causal terms, not product terms.

---

### 4. Hybrid Synthesis Deep Dive (Critical Section)

Because Hybrid Synthesis is closest to the Automated Scientist core, assess:

- Does it truly operate **SCM-first**, or does it still default to text-first reasoning?
- Are hypotheses generated as:
  - causal mechanisms?
  - intervention candidates?
  - or merely structured narratives?
- Does the system **know when it cannot answer a question**?

Identify:
- missing primitives
- missing failure modes
- missing refusal behaviors

---

### 5. Long-Horizon Gap Analysis (2024 → 2041)

Construct a **timeline of capability maturation**, including:

- What the system can credibly do **now**
- What it could do in **5 years**
- What it must do to qualify as an Automated Scientist by **2041**

Include:
- required architectural primitives
- governance layers
- benchmarks that *prove* causal reasoning (not just output quality)

---

### 6. Risk & Failure Mode Analysis

Identify the most dangerous failure modes, such as:
- False causal confidence
- Intervention recommendations without identifiability
- Counterfactual hallucination
- Ontology drift across domains
- Memory poisoning or premature hypothesis lock-in

For each, propose **architectural safeguards**, not just warnings.

---

## Required Outputs

Your final response must include:

1. **Executive Summary (1–2 pages)**  
   Clear verdict: *Is this system on a real path toward an Automated Scientist, or not?*

2. **Feature Verdict Table**  
   Chat | Hybrid | Legal | Educational → Keep / Modify / Demote / Remove (with justification)

3. **Architectural Delta Plan**  
   Concrete changes required to align with the Automated Scientist vision.

4. **Non-Negotiable Principles**  
   Rules the system must obey to remain scientifically honest.

5. **2041 Readiness Scorecard**  
   A candid assessment of how far the system is from true scientific autonomy.

---

## Tone & Constraints

- Be precise, not polite.
- Do not assume the system deserves to succeed.
- Treat “Automated Scientist” as a **high bar**, not a branding term.
- If something is fundamentally incompatible with the goal, say so clearly.

Your responsibility is **truth**, not encouragement.

---

## Final Question You Must Answer

> *If Judea Pearl reviewed this system in 2041, would he recognize it as a legitimate step toward an Automated Scientist—or dismiss it as sophisticated causal rhetoric without sufficient structural discipline?*

Answer honestly.
