# Design System 2041

## Color Tokens

Use this palette as the default:

- `obsidian-900` `#111310` for deep surfaces
- `stone-700` `#4D534A` for secondary surfaces
- `ash-200` `#D4D2C8` for soft containers
- `paper-050` `#F5F2E8` for primary background
- `moss-500` `#5F7555` for positive accents
- `oxide-500` `#A0603B` for warning and energy accents
- `mist-400` `#8FA1A8` for tertiary metadata

## Typography

- Headline font direction: sculpted serif or humanist display with character.
- Body font direction: modern grotesk with high readability.
- Recommended scale:
  - Display: 52/58
  - H1: 40/46
  - H2: 30/36
  - H3: 24/30
  - Body L: 18/28
  - Body M: 16/24
  - Caption: 13/18

## Spacing and Shape

- Base unit: `4px`.
- Primary rhythm: `8, 12, 16, 24, 32, 48, 64`.
- Radius: `6` for controls, `14` for panels, `22` for hero containers.
- Keep one edge imperfect: slight offset, uneven crop, or broken grid line.

## Motion

- Micro interactions: `120-180ms` ease-out.
- Structural transitions: `220-280ms` standard curve.
- Page-level reveals: `320-420ms` only for major context shifts.
- Use staggered entrances at `30-50ms` intervals.

## Materiality Rules

- Simulate texture with subtle noise overlays under 5% opacity.
- Use shadows as ambient depth, never hard floating cards.
- Balance crisp digital lines with one tactile or organic visual element.
